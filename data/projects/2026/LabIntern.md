### New System for Trust Calibration
- Conducted research on a system for trust calibration, with professor [Dae Hyun Kim](https://dhkim16.github.io/) in [Human-Data Interaction Lab.](https://hdil.yonsei.ac.kr/)
- Identified a problem that humans tend to over-rely on AI, and found that previously studied features do not significantly contribute to trust calibration.
- To improve correct-rejection rate, designed a system incorporating source attribution, explanation, and confidence indicators, along with UI improvements.
- Performed a user study to validate the hypothesis.
