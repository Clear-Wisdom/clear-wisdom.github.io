### AI that helps to apply Korean government's business support project
- Hackathon project for [Devpost: Build with Gemini](https://xprize.devpost.com)
- Found that there are so many small-business support project from Korean Government, but small businesses or startup don't know them or don't have a time to write applications.
- Designed a product that helps to find fit support project that has high accept rate for their business, and to write a draft for the application.
