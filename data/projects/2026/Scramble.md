### AI-driven learning support app
- [Scramble](https://doscramble.com)
- Identified that most learning-support software focuses on content comprehension rather than helping users pass exams.
- Converted static learning materials (PDF) into interactive learning experiences using AI - generating roadmaps, quizzes, and flashcards, and tracking proficiency.
- Built a proficiency-scoring algorithm using weighted average calculation.
- Recruited beta-testers and conducted user interviews to define potential feature updates and market fit.
