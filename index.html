About Me

Step 1: Public Administration

I want to contribute to building a society where people are free to pursue what they want to do.
So I started majoring in Public Administration, believing that well-designed policies make society flourish.

But I kept noticing that society rarely moves on its own.
It's too rigid to change until technology pushes it forward.

Step 2: Software Engineering

Therefore, I started studying Software Engineering.
I thought that if I could accelerate technology, and once people start using it, society would leap forward on its own.

But as I built software, I realized something.
The problem wasn't that society was waiting for technology to advance.
The bottleneck was Human Adaptation, the time it took for people to try, adapt, and learn how to use it.

Next Step

Now, I'm interested in how technological advancement can permeate society quickly.
Safely, efficiently, and in forms already familiar to people.
